Our Plans For The Future

Robotics is a new, ever changing, technology. We are always looking for new ideas that will help us grow delicious, fresh produce.

From The Sky

Drones are becoming more readily available for commercial use. We have plans to implement flying drones in a number of roles. to help around the farm, depending on the size of the drone. By using camera drones in conjunction with A.I we soon will be able to analyse fields and monitor crop development. With even larger craft we believe that we will be able to plant and even spray crops.

From The Ground

As Robotics gets more advanced, we can start to implement them in our fields in more roles. So far we have seen how Robots can be used in out fields, but with some limitations. Soon we believe that we will be able to have fully autonomous farms, where the robots will charge, clean and maintain themselves. Advanced A.I will also be able to recognise ripe and fully grown crops, then pick them. With better robots, the produced will be carefully picked, as to not spoil the crops.

From Your Home

With the mass amounts of data each robot will be collecting you will be able to know what is happening with the farm in all aspects. We hope to collect all this data and make it as readable as possible, even from the comfort of your own home while you can take it easy

Endless possibilites

With technology advancing at such a rate, we are excited for what amazing breakthrough will be next. 20 years ago, the use of drones and other robotics in farming was a distant dream. But now robots are able to pick fruit, grow crops and can be an essential piece of equipment on all farms. We will continue to advocate for the use of robotics on farms, to grow a greener future.
