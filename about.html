About Us

We are passionate about the use of robots to help around the farm! we strongly believe that robots are the future of agriculture. In our Robots page you will read about how robots, such as Tom Dick and Wilma, have changed how we farm. We hope to spread the message to persuade other farms to invest in these technologies, to enjoy a more efficient, greener farm..

3 Reasons Why You Should Invest In Robots

  1. Green Farming
  2. Consumer Benefit
  3. Labour Efficiency

Green Farming

Farm automation practices can make agriculture more profitable while also reducing the ecological footprint of farming at the same time. Site-specific application software can reduce the amount of pesticides and fertilizer used while also reducing greenhouse gas emissions.

Consumer Benefit

Consumers preferences are shifting towards organic and sustainably-produced products. With automation technology, produce reaches consumers faster, fresher, and more sustainably. Increase in productivity from automation increases the yield and rate of production, therefore reducing costs for consumers.

Labour Efficiency

Labour is over 50% of the cost to grow a farm and 55% of farmers say they are impacted by labour shortages. Because of this, 31% of farmers are moving to less labour-intensive crops. Routine tasks can be automated with robotics technology, reducing labour costs and manpower. A single strawberry robot harvester has the potential to pick a 25-acre area in 3 days and replace 30 farm workers.
