Tom

We Tom is a scanning robot that can cover 40 Hectares per day. Though not fully automonus just yet, we plan on making him fully autonomus with his own A.I allowing him to charge himself when needed

Dick

We have been able to integrated Rootwave's weed killing technology to make weeding autonomous and even non-chemical to ensure high quality produce. Increasing yeild and reducing the use of chemicals, whilst weeding more effectievly than ever before. We have been able to trail on National Trust estastes. You too could join these trails! .

Wilma

Wilma is able to read and interpret data from Tom and Dick and develop a crop care plan, these will then be carried out by Dick and Tom. Data is always readily available for yourself to read, to help you see how Wilma works and give you confidence in her skills. In addition, you will be able to make your own decisions for Wilma to then relay these to Tom and Dick. This is easily done with a simple web interface.
